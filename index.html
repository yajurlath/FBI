🔍 About This Game

Every day, you eat and drink things that come in a pack. That pack says things on the front. But is it telling you the full story?

The FBI teaches you to read labels like a detective. Check the front. Look at the ingredients. Read the nutrition. Then answer 5 claims and find out what the pack was hiding.

One case a day. Five minutes. A skill you will use for the rest of your life.
